Our Story

Dr. Sharad Salokhe MBBS, MS (Ortho), FRJR, FISHKS is well qualified and skilled Orthopedic Trauma and Robotic Joint Replacement surgeon with 8 years of experience in Orthopedics. He provides an emergency orthopaedic care and is competent in managing simple and complex orthopedic trauma cases.
Dr. Sharad is 1 year fellowship trained surgeon under the aegis of the prestigious Indian Society of Hip Knee Surgeons (ISHKS) in conventional and robotic joint replacement surgery of hip, knee and shoulder. He has also successfully completed fellowship training in Complex Orthopedic Trauma and rehabilitation for 1 year at renowned Amandeep Hospital, Amritsar.

He graduated from RCSM Government Medical College, Kolhapur and has completed his 3 year post graduation in Orthopedics from Government Medical College, Aurangabad. After post graduation, he has worked in the prestigious BJ Medical College, Pune for 1 year. Dr Sharad is currently working as a Consultant in Orthopaedic Trauma and Robotic Joint Replacement surgeon. He provides expert treatment and rehabilitation care for emergency fractures, sports injuries, ligament injuries, joint replacements, pediatric orthopaedics, various shoulder conditions, musculoskeletal problems, arthritis, sciatica and spinal problems at reasonable cost.
